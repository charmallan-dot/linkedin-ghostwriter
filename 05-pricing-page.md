## FAQ Section

### General Questions

**Q: Can I switch plans later?**
A: Yes! Upgrade from Free to Pro anytime. Downgrade or cancel anytime. Pro to Lifetime is also available (we'll credit your remaining time).

**Q: What happens if I exceed the free limit?**
A: You'll see a friendly prompt to upgrade. Your generated posts are still accessible, but you can't generate new ones until next month or until you upgrade.

**Q: Do you offer discounts for annual billing?**
A: Currently we only offer monthly billing for Pro. The Lifetime deal is the best long-term value.

**Q: Can I pay with PayPal?**
A: Yes! PayPal is accepted for Lifetime purchases. Pro is credit card via Stripe.

---

### Pro Plan Questions

**Q: What happens after the 7-day trial?**
A: You'll be charged $15/month automatically. You can cancel before the trial ends to avoid charges.

**Q: Can I cancel anytime?**
A: Yes! Cancel from your dashboard anytime. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

**Q: What if I need more than one account?**
A: Contact us for team/agency pricing. We offer discounts for 5+ seats.

---

### Lifetime Questions

**Q: What does "lifetime" mean?**
A: Lifetime access to LinkedIn Ghostwriter as long as the service exists. We're committed to maintaining this for years to come.

**Q: What if you shut down the service?**
A: If we ever discontinue LinkedIn Ghostwriter, Lifetime users will get 6 months notice and a full refund of their purchase.

**Q: Are updates really free forever?**
A: Yes! All new features, post styles, and improvements are included for Lifetime users at no additional cost.

**Q: Can I gift a Lifetime account?**
A: Yes! Contact us and we'll set up a gift purchase with a custom message.

---

### Technical Questions

**Q: Is my data secure?**
A: Yes. We use Stripe for payments (PCI compliant). We don't store your posts or personal data.

**Q: Do you offer invoicing for companies?**
A: Yes! Pro and Lifetime users can request invoices from their dashboard. Contact support for custom invoicing.

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Pro users have a 30-day money-back guarantee. Lifetime purchases are final after 14 days.
