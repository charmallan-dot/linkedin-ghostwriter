These email sequences are designed to:
1. Welcome new users
2. Drive activation (first post generated)
3. Convert free users to Pro
4. Retain and engage Pro users
5. Win back churned users

**Tools:** Use ConvertKit, MailerLite, or any email platform

## Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips

- Keep it under 50 characters
- Use personalization (first name)
- Create curiosity without clickbait
- A/B test different approaches
- Avoid spam words (free, guarantee, etc.)

### Content Tips

- Write like you're emailing a friend
- Keep paragraphs short (1-3 sentences)
- Use bold for emphasis
- Include ONE clear CTA per email
- Mobile-optimize (most emails opened on phone)

### Timing Tips

- Welcome emails: Send immediately
- Onboarding: Space out (Day 1, 3, 7, 14, 30)
- Promotional: Tuesday-Thursday, 10 AM - 2 PM
- Newsletter: First week of month, consistent day

### Metrics to Track

- Open rate (aim for 40%+)
- Click rate (aim for 5%+)
- Unsubscribe rate (keep under 1%)
- Conversion rate (free → Pro)
- Reply rate (engagement)
